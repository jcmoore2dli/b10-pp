Pronunciation Module

At ILR 2+/3, pronunciation is often the gatekeeper — students may have the vocabulary, the grammar, and the ideas, but delivery failures reduce perceived communicative competence and block score achievement. The pronunciation module addresses this through two parallel pipelines: Whisper for content accuracy and a prosody engine for speech delivery quality. Phase 6 feature — documented here as a preview of platform trajectory.

eg Flat delivery across clauses · no emphasis on key information

Linking, Blending and Schwa
Connected speech features. Consonant linking. Vowel reduction. Schwa in unstressed syllables. Elision and intrusion.
eg "of the" → /əvðə/ not /ɒv ðiː/
eg "and" → /ən/ · "to" → /tə/
〰️
Intonation and Chunking
Thought group boundaries. Rising/falling patterns. Discourse intonation. Meaningful pausing vs. fluency breakdown pausing.
eg "In many cases / the policy fails / due to misaligned incentives" — correct chunking
eg Falling intonation on assertions · rising on questions
Why This Is the Gatekeeper at ILR 2+/3
The Gap
Students at 2+/3 often have sufficient vocabulary and can produce ideas. They fail because delivery reduces perceived communicative competence — poor prosody signals lower proficiency even when content is accurate.
What This Closes
The gap between linguistic competence (what they know) and perceived communicative competence (what the rater hears). Pronunciation feedback ties delivery failures directly to task success outcomes.
Task-Specific Targeting
Paraphrase → chunking and clarity. ESOP → emphatic and rhetorical stress. Hypothesizing → intonation for modality. Narration → fluency and past-tense linking.

Phase 6 — Why This Comes Last
Most complex
Requires a prosody engine, audio alignment layer, and L1 background mapping on top of the existing Whisper + Claude pipeline.
Most costly
Azure Speech or equivalent prosody analysis adds ~$260/year to operating costs for 50 students — the largest single cost increase in the roadmap.
Most impactful
Pronunciation is the gatekeeper at 2+/3. The platform does not fully address the ILR scoring gap until this module is live. Worth doing right.
