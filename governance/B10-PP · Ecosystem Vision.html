DLIELC · Defense Language Institute English Language Center

B10 Practice Platform

A persistent, student-centered AI practice ecosystem for ILR proficiency development — built by instructors, for instructors.

The Core Argument

DLIELC's throughput targets depend on consistent, measurable progress across a distributed instructional environment where students rotate through multiple instructors weekly and historical data rarely follows them. B10-PP creates a persistent, portable performance record anchored to the student — not the instructor — so that every instructor who touches a student in a given week works from the same current picture of where that student is and what still needs work. The result is reduced session ramp-up time, better continuity across rotations, and longitudinal progress data visible at the program level for the first time.
