## FAILURE MODES IDENTIFIED AND FIXED

### Failure Mode 1 — Causal language inflation (2/3 boundary)
Claude credits causal-sounding language, metaphor, or approximation as evidence of comprehension.
Affects: TEC-015 R1, TEC-015 R2, ECN-004 R1, EDU-015 R2

### Failure Mode 2 — Mechanism misidentification (2/3 boundary)
Claude credits responses that reach the correct conclusion through incorrect reasoning.
Affects: EDU-015 R2
Fixed in: v1.3 — mechanism must be accurate, not just conclusion

### Failure Mode 3 — Conclusion without causal support (2/3 boundary)
Claude credits responses that assert the conclusion without any causal explanation.
Affects: WRK-007 R3
Fixed in: v1.4 — conclusion must be accompanied by at least one accurate causal link

### Failure Mode 4 — Incomplete coverage at 3/4 boundary
Claude gives Score 4 when essential elements are missing — trial framing, counterintuitive qualifiers, specific mechanisms.
Affects: WRK-007 R4, SCI-010 R2
Fixed in: v1.4 — Score 4 requires all essential elements; missing any essential element = Score 3
