Option 2 · Best Overall UX Balance
Card Browse with Smart Defaults
Passages displayed as browsable cards. Filters default to what makes most sense for this student based on their history — instructor can override freely. Each card shows the passage question or topic, corpus type, domain, tier, and whether it has been assigned before. One-tap to assign as main or SLT.

Option 5 · Phase 4 Vision · Most Powerful Long-Term
Arc-Driven Suggestions

Requires performance arc data — Phase 4 feature, shown here as a preview of where the platform goes.
